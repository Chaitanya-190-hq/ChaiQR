Good to know

Frequently asked

Does my image get uploaded anywhere?

No. Decoding runs entirely in your browser using a WebAssembly library. Your image never leaves your device.

Can I scan with my camera?

Yes. On the reader page, switch to camera mode and grant permission. The reader continuously scans each frame until a QR code is found.

What image formats are supported?

Any format your browser can render — PNG, JPG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, and SVG rasterised to canvas. Drag-and-drop or file picker both work.

Where is my scan history stored?

In your browser's local storage on this device only. It never syncs to a server, and you can clear it with one click.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The interface is fully responsive and the camera mode uses the rear-facing camera by default on phones.
